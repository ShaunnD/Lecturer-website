Mr. Ikraam Sadek is a passionate Junior Lecturer at Belgium Campus iTversity, dedicated to nurturing the next generation of software developers. With a practical, student‑centred approach, he creates engaging, discussion‑based learning environments where every student is encouraged to think critically, participate actively, and grow into confident, industry‑ready professionals.

Teaching across both foundational and advanced levels, he covers a broad range of disciplines — from first‑year Programming fundamentals to second‑year Web Programming, Software Analysis, Testing, and Information Systems. Beyond the classroom, he channels the same energy he finds on hiking trails into guiding students through complex technical challenges.
